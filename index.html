About Exponentia 2025

Exponentia is the annual mathematics festival organized by the Mathematics Department of NIT Trichy. Bringing together students, educators, and math enthusiasts from across the country, Exponentia 2025 promises a series of engaging events, workshops, and competitions designed to celebrate the beauty and application of mathematics in various fields.

Neural Network Approach: New Frontier in Option Pricing and Financial Decision-Making

Natesan Srinivasan, Department of Mathematics, IIT Guwahati, Guwahati

January 21, 2025 | 02:00 PM - 04:00 PM

In this talk, we focus on the partial differential equations (PDEs) arising in Computational Finance, mainly, the Black-Scholes PDE for European and American options. Obtaining closed form solutions to these PDEs are not possible, and therefore, one has to seek numerical approximate solutions. Here, we implement various types of neural networks, namely, CNN, RNN, Polynomial Neural Networks, Physics-Informed Neural Networks, etc.
